Finance Career Pathways

We strive to be a dynamic, outward facing, and forward looking function with the development of our people at its core.

Use this tool to explore your potential career pathways within Kier. We aim to support and empower our people to prospect the range of finance opportunities across Kier, to build their professional skills and to progress their careers. We do this by providing stretching, interesting work, professional accreditation, career development support, knowledge sharing and access to internal and external networks.

We value the diversity of backgrounds and experience people bring to the function and are building an open, inclusive finance community.

Our Finance Vision

Outstanding people, driving and protecting value through excellent customer service and strong financial stewardship.

People

  • Work as one team, recognising and rewarding behaviours.
  • Attract and invest in outstanding people through training and development.
  • Champion diversity.

Value

  • We help drive business performance and are a catalyst for improvement.
  • We deliver value for the business, adding value through commercial decision support.

Service

  • We build strong relationships with our customers to understand their needs and provide an excellent service.
  • Our information is trusted and timely.

Control

  • We focus on compliance and forward-looking insight.
  • We ensure strong financial controls for sustainable growth.
  • We have clear accountabilities and make decisions at pace.
