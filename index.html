Finance Career Pathways

We strive to be a dynamic, outward facing, and forward looking function with the development of our people at its core.

Use this tool to explore your potential career pathways within Kier. We aim to support and empower our people to prospect the range of finance opportunities across Kier, to build their professional skills and to progress their careers. We do this by providing stretching, interesting work, professional accreditation, career development support, knowledge sharing and access to internal and external networks.

We value the diversity of backgrounds and experience people bring to the function and are building an open, inclusive finance community.
