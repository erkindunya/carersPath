The role

Role Family: Group Finance and Technical Specialisms
Role level: Leader
Knowledge Required
  • Must possess a professional finance qualification such as CIMA, ACCA or ACA
Experience
  • Proven experience of managing teams
  • Experience of 2-3 different job families/ Understanding of different job families and their processes
  • Experience gained in a commercial or management accounting role
  • Experience of leading and developing teams across a number of geographic locations
Necessary Skills
  • Able to develop, motivate and lead a large team
  • Good commercial understanding
  • Strong analytical skills and good judgement
  • Strong verbal and oral communication skills with excellent presentation skillset
  • Delivery focused and pragmatic, able to flex solutions and approach to diverse audiences and business needs
  • Collaborative, able to work with and through others as part of a team and to coach and mentor colleagues
  • Resilient, comfortable with ambiguity and able to prioritise conflicting demands
  • Proficient in MS Office applications (particularly MS Excel)
  • Ability to demonstrate both significant change management and that they have added value to a business

Role progression

The role Head of Financial Planning and Analytics can progress onto strategist level or onto another leader level role.
